### 1 - Company:
- **Table:** `Companies`
  - Fields:
    - `CompanyID`
    - `CompanyName`
    - `Address`
    - `ContactEmail`
    - `ContactPhone`
    - `RegistrationDate`
    - `CEO`

### 2 - Sites/Locations:
- **Table:** `Sites`
  - Fields:
    - `SiteID`
    - `SiteName`
    - `Address`
    - `ContactPhone`
    - `Manager`
    - `Capacity`
    - `OpeningDate`
    - `CompanyID` (foreign key referencing `Companies`)

### 3 - Designation:
- **Table:** `Designations`
  - Fields:
    - `DesignationID`
    - `DesignationName`
    - `DesignationDescription`

### 4 - Team:
- **Table:** `Teams`
  - Fields:
    - `TeamID`
    - `TeamName`
    - `TeamDescription`
    - `DesignationID` (foreign key referencing `Designations`)

### 5 - Menu Master:
- **Table:** `MenuItems`
  - Fields:
    - `MenuItemID`
    - `Category`
    - `Subcategory`
    - `Modifier`
    - `Price`
    - `Seasonal`
    - `Indexing`
    - `ProductOfDay`

### 6 - Taxes Master:
- **Table:** `Taxes`
  - Fields:
    - `TaxID`
    - `TaxType`
    - `Slab`
    - `ItemID` (foreign key referencing `MenuItems`)

### 7 - Ads Master:
- **Table:** `Ads`
  - Fields:
    - `AdID`
    - `Banner`
    - `Text`
    - `Location`

### 8 - Seat Mapping:
- **Table:** `SeatMappings`
  - Fields:
    - `SeatID`
    - `SiteID` (foreign key referencing `Sites`)
    - `SeatNumber`

### 9 - Per seat QR Generation to Seat Mapping:
- **Table:** `SeatQRs`
  - Fields:
    - `QRCode`
    - `SeatID` (foreign key referencing `SeatMappings`)

### 10 - Order Management:
- **Table:** `Orders`
  - Fields:
    - `OrderID`
    - `UserID`
    - `SiteID` (foreign key referencing `Sites`)
    - `QRCode`
    - `Status`
    - `OrderDate`

### 11 - Cash Book/Sales Register:
- **Table:** `Transactions`
  - Fields:
    - `TransactionID`
    - `OrderID` (foreign key referencing `Orders`)
    - `Amount`
    - `TransactionDate`

### 12 - Feedback Management:
- **Tables:** `FeedbackQuestions`, `FeedbackResponses`
  - Fields:
    - **`FeedbackQuestions` Table:**
      - `QuestionID`
      - `QuestionText`

    - **`FeedbackResponses` Table:**
      - `FeedbackID`
      - `UserID`
      - `QuestionID` (foreign key referencing `FeedbackQuestions`)
      - `Comment`
      - `RewardID`

### 13 - Reward/Loyalty Master:
- **Table:** `Rewards`
  - Fields:
    - `RewardID`
    - `RewardName`
    - `LoyaltyPoints`

### User Order:
- **Table:** `UserOrders`
  - Fields:
    - `MobileNumber`
    - `SMSDeliveryStatus`
